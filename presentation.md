# Presentation: Heart Disease Prediction with LangChain

## Slide 1 - Title
Heart Disease Prediction using Machine Learning & LangChain

## Slide 2 - Objective
- Predict heart disease from clinical data
- Provide conversational interface via LangChain

## Slide 3 - Dataset
- UCI Heart Disease dataset (included as heart_disease.csv)
- Features: age, sex, cholesterol, blood pressure, etc.

## Slide 4 - Model & Training
- RandomForestClassifier
- Train/Test split 80/20
- Metrics: accuracy, precision, recall

## Slide 5 - LangChain Integration
- Wrap predictor as a Tool
- Use LLM to accept natural language and call the tool

## Slide 6 - Demo
- Streamlit app / CLI to test predictions

## Slide 7 - Future Work
- Explainability (SHAP)
- Integration with EHRs
- Deployment as web/mobile app
